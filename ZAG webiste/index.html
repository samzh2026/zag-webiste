Professional Brake Solutions

ZAG BRAKES is a leading specialist in the manufacturing of high-performance braking components for the global powersports market. With a focus on Professionalism, High-Performance, and Safety, we provide world-class solutions for all major motorcycle brands.

Our products are engineered to provide a Silent and Comfortable braking experience without compromising on stopping power. Using advanced semi-metallic and sintered formulas, ZAG components ensure extreme durability and stable friction coefficients under the most demanding conditions.

Whether it is on the street or off-road, our mission remains the same: Safety In Every Stop.
